Far Cry 6 is a Ubisoft game and the sixth numerical game in the Far Cry series. It was released on October 7, 2021 for PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X and PC. In addition, it will be released on the Google Stadia streaming platform. The global premiere of Far Cry 6 took place during Ubisoft Forward in July 2020, with gameplay first revealed in late May 2021.

In Far Cry 6, players control Dani Rojas, a guerilla fighter pulled into the war after the death of two of her close companions. After joining Libertad in the fight against Anton Castillo, Dani travels across Yara's island chains to recruit Far Cry 6's other guerilla factions and lead a charge on the capital.
